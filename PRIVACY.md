# AI Firewall — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 27 June 2026_

**The short version:** AI Firewall runs entirely on your device. It makes no
network requests. Your prompts, and anything it detects in them, never leave
your computer and are never sent to us, Anthropic, OpenAI, or anyone else.

## What AI Firewall does
AI Firewall is a browser extension that scans the text you are about to send to
ChatGPT and warns you before confidential or sensitive information leaves your
browser. All detection and redaction happens locally, on your own device.

## What is stored on this device
Two things, both kept only in your browser's local extension storage:
- **Your settings** — the detection toggles, your default action, and the terms
  in your confidential dictionary.
- **A metadata-only activity log** — for each event: the time, the site's
  hostname, what action was taken (blocked, redacted, dismissed, or sent
  anyway), the risk level, how many items were found, and the count per category.

## What is never stored
The text of your prompts and the actual values that are detected are never
written to disk. The activity log records that, for example, "1 email and 1 API
key were found" — never the email address or the key itself.

## What is never collected or transmitted
AI Firewall makes no network requests. It does not collect, sell, or transfer
any user data to us or to any third party. There is no analytics, no telemetry,
and no remote code. Information processed by the extension stays on your device.

## Permissions, and why
- **Storage** — to save your settings and the activity log locally on your device.
- **Access to ChatGPT pages (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)** — to read the
  prompt you are about to send so it can warn you. The extension does not run on
  any other website.

## Your control over your data
- Turn protection off at any time from the popup.
- Clear the entire activity log at any time from the settings page.
- Export your own activity report (CSV or HTML) whenever you want.
- Uninstalling the extension removes everything it stored on your device.

## Children
AI Firewall is a productivity and security tool intended for general and
professional use and is not directed at children.

## Changes to this policy
If the extension's data handling changes, this policy will be updated and the
"Last updated" date above will change.
